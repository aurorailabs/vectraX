## üéØ Search Console Setup (CRITICAL - Do This First!)

### Step 1: Access Google Search Console
1. Visit: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Sign in with your Google account

### Step 2: Add Property
1. Click "Add Property"
2. Select "URL prefix"
3. Enter: `https://vectrax.in/`
4. Click "Continue"

### Step 3: Verify Ownership
You have two options:

**Option A: HTML File (Easiest)**
- You already have `google878ec91e8e6fcb46.html` in your public folder
- Make sure it's uploaded to your Hostinger `public_html` directory
- Google will automatically detect it
- Click "Verify" in Search Console

**Option B: HTML Tag**
- Copy the meta tag from Search Console
- Add it to your `public/index.html` in the `<head>` section
- Rebuild and redeploy
- Click "Verify" in Search Console

### Step 4: Submit Sitemap
1. In Search Console, go to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
2. Enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click "Submit"
4. Wait 24-48 hours for Google to process it

### Step 5: Request Indexing
1. Go to "URL Inspection" tool
2. Enter: `https://vectrax.in/`
3. Click "Request Indexing"
4. Repeat for important pages:
   - `https://vectrax.in/#about`
   - `https://vectrax.in/#services`
   - `https://vectrax.in/#contact`
